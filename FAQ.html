Need a consult?

In order to properly price your next tattoo idea, provide required details:

1. Please provide a description of your tattoo idea along with any reference pics you may have.

2. Include desired Placement on your person (i.e.inside of upper left arm)

3. Describe desired tattoo size in inches (saying small, medium, or large is not an accurate measurement description.

Is a deposit required?

Yes - Consults are free but appointments require a non-refundable deposit of $100 to secure a time and date that works best for your schedule

Why is the deposit non refundable?

1. You forfeit your deposit if you do not show up to your appointment (no call / no show)

2. You are allowed to reschedule 1 time and it must be done within 48 hours in advance. Not doing so will forfeit your deposit

3. If you need to reschedule more than once another deposit will be required

4. Please be on time. If you are late over 1 hour of your scheduled appointment you forfeit your deposit but I also understand Emergencies happen.

5. If you completely change the design upon arrival to your appointment
